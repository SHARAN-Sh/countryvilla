Hotel Country Villa

Our History

Hotel Country Villa was established in 1996 with 8 rooms, one restaurant and a bar. It indeed had a very humble beginning; yet it marked the genesis of a proud and ambitious establishment.

In order to spread its wings, 8 more rooms were added in 1999. 14 deluxe rooms were added in 2003, in conjunction with one conference room with a capacity of 90 people. 2004 turned out to be a yardstick for the hotel with the renovation of the Himalayan Restaurant, addition of coffee shop and expansion of the lobby. At the same time 5 more rooms were added.

To provide a holistic service, Spa, Gym and Bakery shop are in the pipeline to be materialized by 2014. At present, the hotel stands tall in 2.51 acre land.

Cancellation

Our hotel cancellation policy allows free cancellation up to 48 hours before your check-in date. If canceled within this period, no charges will apply. For cancellations made less than 48 hours before check-in or in case of a no-show, a one-night stay will be charged. Please note that certain rates and special offers may have different cancellation policies.

Ensure to review the terms and conditions at the time of booking. We aim to provide flexibility while maintaining fairness to both our guests and the hotel.

Payments

Our hotel payment policy requires a valid credit card at check-in. For standard reservations, the total amount is processed during check-out. If you've booked a prepaid or non-refundable rate, the full payment occurs at the time of booking. We accept major credit cards and may require a pre-authorization for incidental expenses.

Cash payments are accepted with an additional security deposit. Any unpaid charges, such as room service, are settled upon departure. Review specific payment details during the booking process for a seamless check-in experience.

Check In / Out Rules

Check-in time is from 3:00 PM onwards. Early check-in is subject to availability. For guaranteed early arrival, booking the previous night is recommended. Check-out is by 12:00 PM. Late check-out may be arranged for an additional fee, subject to availability. Guests must provide valid identification and a credit card upon check-in.

Payment is processed at check-out, and a pre-authorization may be required for incidental expenses. Please communicate any changes to arrival or departure times in advance for the best possible service.

Disable Access

Our hotel is committed to providing accessible accommodations. We offer wheelchair-accessible entrances, elevators, and designated parking spaces.

Accessible rooms feature wider doorways, grab bars, and roll-in showers. Our staff is trained to assist with specific needs. Please inform us in advance for a comfortable and tailored stay.

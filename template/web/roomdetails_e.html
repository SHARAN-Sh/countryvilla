Executive Suite Room

Spacious luxurious Executive Suite Room located on the topmost floor.

With high ceiling, tented roof and two large balconies facing directly towards the Himalayan range, offers king sized bed, a living room, a two person in-room jacuzzi, and all other amenities that will make your stay a remarkable one. The room was recently renovated using only high quality materials and locally sourced reclaimed wood to give it a rustic feel.

The dark wood panelling and furnishings, deluxe red-draped four-poster bed, and magnificent black stone bathroom evoke the charm.
